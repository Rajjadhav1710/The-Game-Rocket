What Is Flappy Bird ?

Flappy Bird Is a side-scrolling mobile game featuring 2D retro style graphics.

The objective was to direct a flying bird, named "Faby", who moves continuously to the right, between sets of Mario-like pipes.

If the player touches the pipes, they lose.

Faby briefly flaps upward each time that the player taps the screen. if the screen is not tapped, Faby falls because of gravity

each pair of pipes that he navigates between earns the player a single point, with medals awarded for the score at the end of the game.

White medal is awarded to scores less than or equal to 10.

A bronze medal is given to scores between 11 and 20.

In order to receive the silver medal, the player must have point between 21 and 40.

The gold medal is given to those who score higher than 40 points.
