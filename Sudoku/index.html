  • Tip 1: Look for rows, columns of 3Ã—3 sections that contain 5 or more numbers. Work through the remaining empty cells, trying the numbers that have not been used.   • Tip 2: Break the grid up visually into 3 columns and 3 rows. Each large column will have 3, 3Ã—3 grids and each row will have 3, 3Ã—3 grids.
